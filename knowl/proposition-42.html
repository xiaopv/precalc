Task 5.11.

Let \(f(x) = \frac{x^2+x-2}{x^2-x-6}\text{.}\)
  1. Find the domain and the range of \(f(x)\text{.}\)
  2. Find all the vertical and horizontal asymptotes of \(f(x)\text{.}\)
  3. Find the intervals that \(f(x)\) is increasing and the intervals that \(f(x)\) is decreasing.
  4. What is the average rate of change for \(4 \lt x \lt 8\text{?}\)
  5. Solve the inequality \(f(x) \gt 6\text{.}\)
