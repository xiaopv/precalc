Task 4.9.

Use your calculator and pick values for \(A, B\) and \(r\) to test whether each of the following is true. If the statement is false, cross it out so that you do not attempt to apply it.
  1. For every positive numbers \(A, B\text{,}\) \(\log_b(AB) = \log_b(A) + \log_b(B)\text{.}\)
  2. For every positive numbers \(A, B\text{,}\) \(\log_b(A+B) = \log_b(A) + \log_b(B)\text{.}\)
  3. For every positive number \(A\) and every number \(r\text{,}\) \(\log_b(A^r) = r\log_b(A)\text{.}\)
  4. For every positive numbers \(A, B\text{,}\) \(\log_b(\frac{A}{B}) = \log_b(A) - \log_b(B)\text{.}\)
  5. For every positive numbers \(A, B\text{,}\) \(\frac{\log_b(A)}{\log_b(B)} = \log_b(A) - \log_b(B)\text{.}\)
  6. For every positive numbers \(A, B\text{,}\) \(\log_b(A - B) = \log_b(A) - \log_b(B)\text{.}\)
