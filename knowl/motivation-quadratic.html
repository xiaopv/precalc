Task 2.1.

Suppose that you are in charge of managing constructing materials at a constructing site. You want to build walls to enclose a rectangular area to store the constructing materials needed for project and you want to have as much area as possible. However, you only have enough materials to build \(180\) feet of walls.
  1. Pick your favorite length and width so that you can use all the building materials. Then calculate the area of the region.
  2. Pick your second favorite length and width and repeat the process of the last question. Which dimension gives larger area?
  3. Draw a diagram showing the rectangular area, and labeling the length of the region using a variable \(\ell\text{.}\) Then find the width of the region in terms of the length\(\ell\text{.}\)
  4. Write a function equation describing the area of the rectangular area \(A\) in terms of the length \(\ell\text{.}\) Use Desmos to graph this function.
  5. Using the graph of this function, decide how should the wall be built to get the maximum possible area. What is the maximum possible area?
