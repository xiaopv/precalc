Task 3.8.

Pam opens an investment account at Vanguard with \($10,000\text{.}\) The account earns approximately \(6.5\%\) compounded annually. Let \(t\) be the number of years the account has been open, and \(B(t)\) the balance in the account.
  1. Make a table showing the balance, \(B(t)\text{,}\) in the account at \(t=0,1,2,3,\) and \(4\) years.
  2. Write a function equation for the function \(B(t)\text{.}\)
  3. What is the domain and the range of the function \(B(t)\text{?}\)
  4. Use your function equation to determine the balance in \(20\) years.
  5. For the balance of the account to reach \($100,000\text{,}\) at least how many years should Pam wait?
